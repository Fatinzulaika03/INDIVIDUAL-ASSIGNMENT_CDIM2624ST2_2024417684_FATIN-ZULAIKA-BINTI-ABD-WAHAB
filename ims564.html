Course Information

IMS564 - User Experience Design

Course Description

This course introduces students to apply User Experience (UX) design principles in designing a product or service of their creation. It enables students to analyze and design properly UX on respective projects and cover one or more of the UX methods and tools in their potential design solutions. The topics covered in this course are Introduction to UX; UX Ecosystem; Business Requirements and User Needs; Creating Personas; The Strategy, Scope, Structure, Skeleton and Surface Plane; and Transition: From Design to Development and Beyond. The teaching methods are lecture, discussion, and hands on. Students will be assessed through test, assignment and presentation.
